FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need any technical skills?+
Not at all. SurveySphere runs entirely in your browser — open index.html and you're ready. No installation, no server, no configuration needed.
What AI model powers SurveySphere?+
Groq via the Groq API. You need a free API key from https://console.groq.com — it takes under 2 minutes to set up and is free with generous usage limits. Add it once in the Settings panel.
How does the free plan work?+
The free plan gives you 10 AI-generated surveys with all 6 question types, unlimited responses, basic analytics, and CSV export. No credit card needed to get started.
Where is my data stored?+
All surveys and responses are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any external server except for AI generation. Your data stays with you.
Can I edit AI-generated surveys?+
Yes, absolutely. Every AI-generated survey is fully editable. Add questions, remove them, reorder, or change types — the AI output is just a starting point.
What does the TXT export include?+
TXT reports include the survey title, export date, total responses, and every individual response with question text, answers, submission timestamp, and completion time — all in a clean plain-text format.
